Who We Are

Project Whistler is an open-source database that shows the records about the intensity of sound inside the Dhaka Metropolitan Area.

The AlgoJect team runs Project Whistler through the help of some kind-hearted contributors across Dhaka City. The work is done in four steps.

SUBMISSION

Our team or the volunteers first go to particular regions and assemble the pieces of information about the noise of that regions. They then send us specific documents through a third-party app with proof of that info and their own identity.

VERIFICATOIN

In the second step, we ensure all the evidence. If our team thinks that the information is correct by looking at it from different sources or talking to the sender in a special case, then we can perform the verification and select to send that info to the server.

LAUNCHING

In the next step, we upload the verified documents to our server. We enrich our Project Whistler by uploading appropriate documents to the specific profiles of different areas at regular intervals.

VISUALIZATOIN

Finally, all our collaborators and visitors can see verified and accurate information from our database.
